Nigerian Jollof Pasta

This dish is a delicious fusion of the popular Nigerian Jollof Rice. Below are the ingredients for preparing this meal.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Tomato Paste
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Veg.Oil
  • Bay leaves, Curry powder, Thyme
  • Seasonings
  • Salt

Procedure

  • Step1: Boil water, then par boil the spaghetti for 5mins
  • Step2: Blend the onion and pepper together
  • Step3: Fry the pepper mix along with tomato paste for 5mins
  • Step4: Add the spicies e.g. bay leaves, curry powder, thyme
  • Step5: Add Seasonings and Salt to taste
  • Step6: Add the spaghetti and allow to cook until it absorbs the sauce
  • Step7: Serve and enjoy with any side dish of choice

Alfredo Pasta

This dish is popular known by the Itailians.It can be served with chicken or sea food.Below are the ingredients for preparing this meal.

Ingredients

  • Penny pasta
  • Cream
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Procedure

  • Step1: Boil water, then par boil the pasta for 5mins
  • Step2: Melt butter and cream together on low heat
  • Step3: Add garlic, seasoning and salt to taste
  • Step4: Add cheese and stir
  • Step5: Toss the pasta in the cheese sauce and allow to cook for 3mins
  • Step7: Serve and enjoy with any side dish of choice
